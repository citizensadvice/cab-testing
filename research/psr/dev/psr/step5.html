Priority Services Register

Your results

You can claim the following additional help from the priority services register.

Accessible bills

Suppliers can provide customers with account and bill information in an accessible format, e.g. with larger print or braille.

Would you like accessible bills?

Advanced notice of planned power cuts

Advance notice of planned power cuts for customers who depend on their energy supply for medical reasons.

Would you like advanced notice of planned power cuts?

Priority in an emergency

This could include providing alternative heating and cooking facilities in the event of supply disconnection.

Would you like priority in an emergency?

Password protection

Provide reassurance that callers, for example meter readers, are genuine.

Would you like password protection?

Bill nominee scheme

Customers can ask their supplier to send their bill to anybody (for example a family member or carer) who has agreed to receive it.

Would you like to be part of the bill nominee scheme?

Prepayment meters

If a customer is unable reach to their prepayment meter they can asked for it to be moved.

Would you like a prepayment meter?

Quarterly meter reading services

If no person occupying the premises is able to read the meter.

Would you like quarterly meter reading services?

Annual gas appliance safety checks for homeowners

If you do not own your own home your landlord is responsible for ensuring that any gas appliances, fittings and flues are safe.

Would you like annual gas appliance safety checks?
