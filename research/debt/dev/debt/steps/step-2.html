Step 2 - Work out which debts are a priority

This advice is for

When you have more than one debt, you must make sure you’re dealing with the most important ones first.

These are your ‘priority debts’.

Priority debts are the ones that have the most serious consequences - like being evicted, losing your house or getting a court fine.

Use our tool below to see which of your debts is a ‘priority’ debt. This will show you how to decide which ones to deal with first.
